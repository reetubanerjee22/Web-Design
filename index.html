What is Danceslay?

Danceslay is run by two self taught dancers and their manager. We conduct dance classes for everybody who is an eager learner. The best part about us is that we charge a very less amount and for underpriviledged people its free of cost. Danceslay offers professional dance lessons for beginners and advanced students. We teach Hip Hop, kathak, bharatnatyam, semi classical, freestyle, jazz, ballet, zumba and many more.The world's best dance learning academy is now at your fingertips. Join our dance classes online and keep your technique sharp from the comfort of home. We also conduct offline classes for the students who can visit our centres across India. So, what are you waiting for? Register now and enjoy our interactive dance lessons and become a better dancer.

About Us
